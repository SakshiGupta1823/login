  • About Us
  • Mughal Carpet Industries was established in 1978 and has ever since been a leading export house of the country. We manufacture and export Persian carpets, durries and Shaggy Rugs across India and abroad.Based in Bhadohi, Uttar Pradesh Mughal Carpets Industries has created a credible reputation in the market through these years. Our first buyer was from Belgium and there after we have been covering nation after nation, the Chinese market being the latest.
